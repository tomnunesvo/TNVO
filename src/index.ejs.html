About

Smooth, balanced, complex, with hints of spice and nutty undertones

For over three decades, Tom has been an actor, singer, software designer, corporate denizen, husband and father. He draws on his broad range of experience to produce a sound that blends authority, trustworthiness, and credibility with approachable likability.

Tom is known for his professionalism and dedication to the quality of his work. His focus is always the success of your project. Tom takes his work seriously, but never himself. You will find him a pleasure to work with and good for a few laughs.

Sourced from his home studio in the Hartford, CT area, Tom produces high quality digital recordings with fast turnaround. And he is just a 2-3 hour commute from NYC and Boston.
